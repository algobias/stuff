Tournament Format

Pool Play Format

4-Team Pools:

  • Best of 3 sets
  • Non-deciding sets: First to 25 points (2-point advantage, no cap)
  • Deciding set: First to 15 points (2-point advantage, no cap)
  • Rally scoring system
  • No side changes

3-Team Pools:

  • 3 sets played to 25 (regardless of outcome)
  • 2-point advantage required (no cap)
  • Rally scoring system
  • No side changes
